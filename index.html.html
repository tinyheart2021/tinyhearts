About Tiny Heart


At Tiny Heart , we believe every child deserves love, care, and an opportunity to thrive.
Our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for orphaned and abandoned children while empowering them to build bright futures.

Tiny Heart is a non-profit organization.
We are dedicated to supporting children who have lost their families or been displaced due to unforeseen circumstances.
With a team of passionate caregivers, educators, and volunteers, we ensure every child receives the care and education they deserve.

- Provide shelter, meals, and healthcare to children in need.
- Offer educational programs to prepare children for a successful future.
- Create a loving community where children feel valued and supported.
- Partner with individuals and organizations to facilitate adoption and foster care.
